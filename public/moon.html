Moon

"The Moon, Earth's only natural satellite, has been a source of wonder and inspiration for millennia. Its silvery glow lights up our night skies, guiding travelers and stirring poets and scientists alike. With its phases, the Moon influences the rhythms of life on Earth, from the tides to ancient calendars. The surface of the Moon, with its craters and maria, tells a story of cosmic history and violent impacts. Human missions to the Moon have expanded our understanding of the universe and opened the door to further space exploration. As we gaze up at the Moon, we are reminded of our place in the vast cosmos and the endless possibilities of discovery."
